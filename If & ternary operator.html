The ballad of the witches road

Agatha though times version

Down, down, down the road, down the witches road

Down, down, down the road, down the witches road

Down, down, down the road, down the witches road

Circle sewn with fate

Unlock thy hidden gate

***

Merching ever forward neeth the wooden shrine

I stray not from the path, i hold death's hand in mine

Primal night, given sight, familiar by thy side

If one be gone we carry on

spirit as our guide

Down, down, down the road, down the witches road

Down, down, down the road, down the witches road

Down, down, down the road, down the witches road

Blood and tears and bone

Maiden, mother, crone

***

The road is wild and wicked, wilding to the wood

Where all there's wrong is right and all there's all bad is good

Though many miles of tricks and trials, we'll wonder high and low

Tame your fear and doors appears, the time has come to go

Down, down, down the road, down the witches road

Down, down, down the road, down the witches road

Down, down, down the road, down the witches road

Follow me my friends

To glory and the end

Agatha harkness, Jennifer kale, Lilia calderou, Alice wu, Mrs. hart
